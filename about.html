About Us

Building Trust Through Excellence Since 1983

Company Overview

Kunjal Synergies Private Limited has been a cornerstone in India's petrochemical industry since 1983. As a leading importer and distributor of Petrochemicals, Solvents, and Pharma Raw Materials, we've built our reputation on reliability, quality, and innovation.

With our headquarters in Kolkata and strategic presence across major Indian cities, we serve diverse industries with comprehensive solutions in chemical distribution and supply chain management.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted partner in petrochemical distribution, setting industry standards for quality, reliability, and sustainable practices.

Our Mission

To deliver superior value to our stakeholders through innovative solutions, operational excellence, and sustainable business practices.
